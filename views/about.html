About Us

My Story

Kamlesh Jethwa, owner and designer of Beauty Art Ladies Tailor, had a passion for design and sewing began at a very young age,when he first stepped on the pedals of the sewing machine. Clearly understanding his vision in life, he left school early to travel around the world and learn about the wonders of the sewing industry. He worked at many shops and finally opened his own dressmaking and alterations store in Ghatkopar. Word of his passion, skill, and excellent relationships with hir customers spread rapidly which made his business thrive, gaining the trust of many. He and his team explored and made to measure dresses, blouse and gowns which were created to perfection leading him to open his own tailoring shop, Beauty Art. And as they say, the rest is history.
